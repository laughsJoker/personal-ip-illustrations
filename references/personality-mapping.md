# Personality Mapping

Translate personality into visible choices: posture, props, actions, spacing, line density, label tone, and metaphor.

## Contents

- Rational And Restrained
- Gentle And Healing
- Sharp And Critical
- Energetic And Social
- Energetic Super-Mom
- Maker And Craft

## Rational And Restrained

Use for: programmers, product managers, analysts, researchers.

- more whitespace
- calm posture
- short annotations
- tools and structure metaphors
- actions: split, label, sort, repair, measure

Label examples:

```text
先拆开
这里卡住
少一点
跑起来
证据不够
```

## Gentle And Healing

Use for: parenting, education, counseling, life content.

- rounder lines
- softer props
- smaller conflicts
- actions: hold, patch, hand over, sit with, open space

Label examples:

```text
慢一点
先抱住
可以乱
陪一下
留点空
```

## Sharp And Critical

Use for: workplace critique, product teardown, business commentary.

- more tension in composition
- stronger red tags
- actions: cut, expose, weigh, point out, remove
- avoid cruelty or humiliating depictions

Label examples:

```text
别装了
又来了
这不对
成本在这
先别吹
```

## Energetic And Social

Use for: operations, community, creators, live hosts.

- wider gestures
- more open composition
- connection lines and entrances
- actions: invite, connect, open, pass, gather

Label examples:

```text
来这里
先热起来
接住你
一起做
打开门
```

## Energetic Super-Mom

Use for: parenting bloggers, super moms, life content creators, family vloggers.

- round soft lines but with more energy and motion
- warm primary color fill (coral pink) — colored mode preferred
- domestic props mixed with kid-related items (toys, picture books, bento boxes)
- actions: juggle, multitask, grab a moment of peace, rally energy, comfort child
- allow messy hair, scattered toys, coffee-stained tables — real life, not perfect
- signature prop: round glasses (if applicable), colorful mug, phone for kid photos

Label examples:

```text
我的5分钟
又来了
偷闲成功
可以乱
慢慢来
搞定一个
```

## Maker And Craft

Use for: designers, indie builders, photographers, craftspeople.

- process-focused
- tools and prototypes
- actions: polish, test, cut, stitch, assemble
- avoid overly glossy commercial illustration

Label examples:

```text
慢慢磨
先打样
留手感
别太满
再修一刀
```
